Interference Series I

Price: $1800
Materials: Mixed media on canvas
Year: 2024
Size: 36" x 48"
This artwork explores the tension between color and motion. Using an innovative layering technique, it creates a visual effect that shifts as the viewer moves. The work embodies a dynamic sense of transformation, engaging the viewer through both color and form.

Gathering Light

Gathering Light is a permanent interactive LED installation installed at Holy Innocents' Episcopal School. This work was installed in 2019 and has since been used in various courses to enhance Computer Science and Art education.

View Project

Repeat Offfender

Repeat Offfender was my brand of digitally produced products from 2014 to 2019. Over 80 boutiques and museum gift shops carried my line of 3D Printed Jewelry including shopSCAD, Renwick Gallery, Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Arts, High Museum of Art, FIDM Museum, and the Henry Ford Museum. In 2018, Repeat Offfender expanded to produce knitwear, including a made-to-order custom sweater generator.

About Jordan

Jordan Oleson-Graves is an Atlanta-based artist and designer making art with code in a variety of media, including jewelry, textiles, and interactive installations. She graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design with a BFA in Motion Media Design and Georgia Tech with an MS in Digital Media, where her research included the relationship between crafting and computer science education, novel interfaces for digital and personal fabrication, and creative social interactions through digital media. She is currently pursusing an MFA in Painting at the Savannah College of Art and Design.
