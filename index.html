Community Garden

Community Garden is an immersive, digital installation where participants are invited to draw and plant a flower via a customized Garden website http://communitygarden.jordangraves.com
With a simple swipe, the flower appears in a publicly projected field of flowers.

Gathering Light

Gathering Light is a permanent interactive LED installation installed at Holy Innocents' Episcopal School. This work was installed in 2019 and has since been used in various courses to enhance Computer Science and Art education.

View Project

Repeat Offfender

Repeat Offfender was my brand of digitally produced products from 2014 to 2019. Over 80 boutiques and museum gift shops carried my line of 3D Printed Jewelry including shopSCAD, Renwick Gallery, Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Arts, High Museum of Art, FIDM Museum, and the Henry Ford Museum. In 2018, Repeat Offfender expanded to produce knitwear, including a made-to-order custom sweater generator.

About Jordan

Jordan Graves is an Atlanta-based artist and designer making art with code in a variety of media, including jewelry, textiles, and interactive installations. She graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design with a BFA in Motion Media Design. She is currently pursuing a PhD in Digital Media at Georgia Tech where her research includes the relationship between crafting and computer science education, novel interfaces for digital and personal fabrication, and creative social interactions through digital media.
